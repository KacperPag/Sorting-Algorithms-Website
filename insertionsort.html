Insertion Sort

Insertion sort works by building up a sorted sublist in the first part of the original list, while the remaining part of the list remains unsorted. At the start, the sorted sublist contains just a single item (the first item in the list). All of the other items belong to the unsorted sublist. The algorithm goes through the unsorted sublist, item by item. As each item is examined, it is moved into the correct position (in ascending or descending order) in the sorted sublist. This progresses until the final item is correctly inserted and the list is sorted.
